Fortitude Broadband Service Agreement

This Agreement is entered into by and between Fortitude Broadband, hereinafter referred to as the "Service Provider," and our intending customer, hereinafter referred to as the "Subscriber" or "Customer," collectively referred to as the "Parties."

1. Service Description:

1.1 The customer must submit a service request form after selecting their preferred service from the provider website.

1.2 The Service Provider agrees to provide Internet services to the Subscriber.

1.3 The service includes high-speed internet access, with the specific plan details outlined in the accompanying service plan document.

1.4 The Service Provider will use reasonable efforts to ensure the continuous availability and reliability of the internet service.

2. Service Installation:

2.1 The Service Provider will install the necessary equipment for the internet service at the Subscriber's premises in accordance with the agreed-upon installation schedule.

2.2 Special request from the customer on the use of materials not covered by the installation fees will be paid for as extra charges by the customer.

2.3 The Subscriber agrees to provide access to their premises to facilitate the installation process.

2.4 The Service Provider shall work at the Customer’s premises Monday through Saturday from 8:00 am to 6:00 pm, excluding Sundays and Public Holidays. The Service Provider may deny Customer requests for work outside these hours.

Fortitude Broadband shall be solely responsible for the purchase, installation, operation, maintenance and repair of all their equipment and facilities required in connection with the Services under this Agreement.

3.1 All Hardware installed belongs to Fortitude Broadband and Fortitude Broadband reserves the right to retrieve the hardware upon termination, excluding those purchased or paid for by the Customer.

3.2 In the event of any hardware failure/damage as a result of the Customer’s negligence caused by any of the underlying factors, the hardware shall be replaced by Fortitude Broadband at an agreed fee to be paid by the Customer:

  • i.Damage to the System(s) caused by Customer or a third Party acting on behalf of Customer.
  • ii.Acts or attempts to move, reinstall, repair, maintain or modify the System(s) by non-Fortitude Broadband authorized personnel.
  • iii.Scheduled maintenance and upgrades on the LAN by customer.
  • iv.When equipment are not protected with surge suppressors, lightning arrestors, grounding facility, uninterruptible power supplies (UPS) with Automatic Voltage Regulators (AVR) and other devices which are required for the supply of uninterrupted and “clean” electrical power.

4. Service Fees:

4.1 The Subscriber agrees to pay the service fees as specified in the selected service plan.

4.2 Service fees are billed monthly and are due on the date specified in the invoice.

5. Billing and Payment:

5.1 The Service Provider will provide invoices to the Subscriber electronically.

5.2 Payments must be made by the due date to avoid service interruption.

6. Service Maintenance:

6.1 The Service Provider will perform routine maintenance to ensure the optimal performance of the internet service.

6.2 The Subscriber will be notified in advance of any scheduled maintenance that may temporarily affect service availability.

7. Service Support:

7.1 The Service Provider will provide technical support to address issues related to the internet service.

7.2 Technical support can be reached through the contact details provided by the Service Provider.

8.1 Either party may terminate this agreement with writing a 30-day notice ahead, if the other party breaches any material term of this agreement.
